About Savory Bistro

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Savory Bistro has been serving exquisite cuisine in a warm and elegant atmosphere. Our mission is to provide an exceptional dining experience through a combination of delectable food, impeccable service, and a welcoming ambiance.

Our Chef

Executive Chef Michael Laurent brings over 20 years of culinary expertise from renowned restaurants across Europe. His innovative approach to classic cuisine creates unique flavors that delight our guests daily.

Our Ingredients

We believe in using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients. We partner with regional farmers and producers to ensure every dish represents the best seasonal offerings available.

Awards & Recognition

  • Best Fine Dining 2022 - City Culinary Awards
  • Chef's Excellence Award 2021
  • Wine Selection of the Year 2020
